Vegas Astoria Hotel & Suites — Redefining Hospitality in Onitsha

Vegas Astoria Hotel & Suites opens to redefine hospitality and style in Onitsha, Anambra State, Nigeria. Strategically located at No. 12B Vegas Astoria Boulevard (former Ridge Road), opposite the Stock Exchange, GRA Onitsha, we offer world-class facilities and services that rank among the best in Nigeria.

Our property features 72 elegantly furnished executive rooms, suites, and apartments; the 6th Floor Top Roof Niger Bistro Bar overlooking the majestic Niger River; Astoria Gourmet Restaurant with fine dining and themed nights; and the exclusive 6th Floor Aqua Lounge for private luxury lounging. Guests enjoy an underground gym and spa, an ultra-modern swimming pool with Variety Nights on Thursdays and Saturdays, plus Sunday brunch and dinner buffets. We also host events in our 800-capacity Aficionado Hall and two mini conference halls (50- and 100-capacity).

Additional amenities include ample underground parking, 24-hour CCTV surveillance with armed security, uninterrupted power supply, high-speed fiber internet, and a cyber cafe/business center — ensuring comfort, safety, and convenience throughout your stay.
